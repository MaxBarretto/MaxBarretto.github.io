HEALTH 

Learn proper health practices for gamers

HEALTH PLAN

FOOD PLAN

There are five main food groups that you want to be fulfilling when planning a diet: Fruits, Vegetables, Grains, Protein Foods, and Dairy. Keeping a good balance is key to staying healthy. Just eating all of these foods is a starting place, but staying healthy means becoming consistent with your diet and getting into a routine. Foods with lots of sugar and fat may taste good, but you will expend the energy you get from them faster.

ACTIVITY PLAN

In order to keep your muscles and joints healthy, you need to use stretches to prevent aches and sores, but also to keep unused muscles healthy after long gaming sessions. Some easy stretches and activities to try are squats, lunges, push-ups, crunches, or just simply walking. The main goal of working out is to burn calories from the foods you eat to keep your metabolism up, which can help your gaming performance tremendously.
